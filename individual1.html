Problem 5

Find out how income affects the ownership of an individuals home (i.e.owns or rents).

ggplot(data, aes(x = Rent)) + geom_bar() + coord_flip() + facet_wrap( ~ Income) + xlab('No. of Individuals') + ylab('Categotry of Home Ownership') + ggtitle('The No. of Individuals in each Housing and Income Bracket')

We observe that people the most people who buy a house have a income of $250000 or more. However, people with low income also consider owning or buying a house. This gives us an idea that many people might see buying a house as a long term investment which can be passed down to many generations. At the same time, we cant generalize these observations since a significant amount of people chose not to answer in this survey.
